Link Poster for Telegram

Quick & easy link sharing to your Telegram groups

How to Use

  • 1 Right-click on any link on a webpage to see the option Post this link to Telegram
  • 2 Right-click anywhere else on a page to see Post page URL to Telegram
  • 3 A popup will appear showing the URL and a group selector
  • 4 Select your target group and click Post

Configuration

The extension posts links to a Telegram Groups related to (gyanmarg.guru)

To Enable:

  • First join the Telegram Group given in the list
  • Login to gyanmarg.guru as usual using your email.
  • If you are already logged in, there is no need to login again.
